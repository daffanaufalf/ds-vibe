# Typography

Typography is used to display text content in a clear and consistent manner across the application. It maps directly to our design tokens for font size, weight, and line height.

## Text

The `Text` component is used for body content, captions, and labels. It is polymorphic and defaults to a `<p>` element.

<Canvas of={TypographyStories.TextScale} />

## Heading

The `Heading` component is used for titles and subtitles. It supports semantic levels `h1` through `h6`.

## Accessibility

- **Semantic HTML**: Always use `Heading` with the correct level (`as="h1"`, etc.) to maintain a proper document structure.
- **Contrast**: All default text colors against their respective backgrounds meet WCAG 2.1 AA requirements.
- **Hierarchy**: Use consistent sizing and weights to establish a clear information hierarchy for screen reader users.
